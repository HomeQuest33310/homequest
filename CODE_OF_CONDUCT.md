# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the HomeQuest community welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone.

HomeQuest is a project built for families and children. Kindness is one of our core values.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes being respectful, welcoming newcomers, giving constructive feedback, helping others learn, listening before judging, and respecting different cultures and family structures.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, hate speech, discrimination, bullying, trolling, sharing private information without consent, and sexual or violent content unrelated to the project are not tolerated.

## Family-Friendly Community

HomeQuest aims to remain appropriate for all ages. Contributors should avoid offensive language, inappropriate jokes, violent imagery, and content unsuitable for children.

## Privacy

Never request or publish personal information about children or families.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or reject content that violates this Code of Conduct. Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and adapted to HomeQuest.
