# Adventurers and Mercenaries Guide

[Version française](GUIDE_AVENTURIERS_MERCENAIRES_FR.md)

Welcome to HomeQuest. Your goal is simple: complete real-life missions, improve your hero, and help your Kingdom reach shared goals.

## Adventurer or Mercenary?

| Role | How it works |
| --- | --- |
| **Adventurer** | A permanent Kingdom member who takes part in quests and collective progress without a planned end date. |
| **Mercenary** | A member invited for a limited time. Access may cover the whole Kingdom or only one specific Domain. |

A Domain-limited Mercenary can only select quests from that Domain. Their temporary access ends when the invitation period expires.

## The game loop

1. Open the **Grand Quest Register**.
2. Take an available mission or open **My Missions**.
3. Complete the task in real life.
4. Submit it for validation, with a note when useful.
5. Once approved, collect your gains and contribute to the Kingdom’s goals.

## Understanding quests

- **One time**: the quest does not return after completion.
- **Daily**: each participant can complete it once per day.
- **Weekly**: each participant can complete it once per week.

A daily or weekly assignment stays attached to your profile. At the start of the next period, it automatically becomes available again; a Guardian does not need to reassign it.

After completion for the current period, it appears under **Completed for this period**. You may leave a recurring assignment with **Leave mission**, unless a completion is still waiting for validation. Previous completions and rewards are kept.

Some quests are scheduled. Until their date and time are reached, the take-mission button remains disabled and the app shows when the quest will become available.

## Validation and gains

Depending on the quest, validation may be automatic or require a Guardian’s approval. The Guardian can:

- approve the mission;
- ask you to try again and provide an encouraging explanation.

An approved quest may grant:

- **XP** and levels;
- personal **gold**;
- experience in one or more **skills**;
- damage against the active **Boss**;
- a contribution to Kingdom resources and collective goals.

Personal gold can notably be used to unlock new profile avatars.

## Suggesting your own initiative

From **level 10**, an Adventurer or Mercenary can suggest an initiative from the quest register:

1. choose an idea from the catalogue or prepare your proposal;
2. select the relevant Domain;
3. add a note if needed;
4. send the request to the Guardians.

Once accepted, the initiative becomes a mission assigned to you. A Domain-limited Mercenary may only submit initiatives for that Domain.

## Helping the Kingdom

Every approved quest may have several collective effects:

- advance the group’s **highest-priority reward**;
- damage the active **Boss**;
- earn wood, stone, or provisions;
- count toward evolution buildings unlocked by completed quests.

Guardians choose the reward order. Priority 1 receives progress first; when it is completed, later quests automatically advance the next priority.

Kingdom reserves fund other buildings. You help fill the reserves, but only Guardians decide on spending, crystal conversions, and construction work.

## Other useful areas

- **Profile**: view your level, gold, skills, titles, trophies, and avatars.
- **Hall of Heroes**: see how Kingdom members are progressing.
- **Boss**: follow the collective enemy’s health and planned rewards.
- **Reward Wishes**: suggest a real-world group reward and follow accepted, delivered, or archived goals.
- **Supplies**: add an item, volunteer to get it, mark it as brought home, or put it back on the list.
- **Kingdom Legend**: revisit the milestones of your family adventure.

## Good habits

- Only take missions you reasonably expect to complete.
- Add a note when the situation needs context.
- Leave a recurring mission if you no longer want it assigned.
- Use reward wishes to suggest goals that motivate the whole group.
- Tell a Guardian if your Mercenary access, Domain, or a mission looks incorrect.

## Quick questions

**My daily or weekly quest disappeared from “To do.”**

Look under **Completed for this period**. It will return automatically in the next period.

**Can I complete a daily quest several times on the same day?**

No. The limit applies separately to each participant.

**Why can’t I take a quest?**

It may already be completed for this period, scheduled for later, outside your Mercenary Domain, or waiting for validation.

**Who decides how Kingdom resources are used?**

Guardians do. Adventurers and Mercenaries earn those resources through quests.
